PaXSeries

PaXSeries is an option for PaXEvaluate. It allows to expand Passarino-Veltman functions around given variables.

The expansion is done by LoopRefineSeries and the syntax is the same as with the ordinary Series, e.g.Â PaXSeries -> {{m0,0,2}} or PaXSeries -> {{SPD[p1,p1],pp1,0},{SPD[p2,p2],pp2,0}}.

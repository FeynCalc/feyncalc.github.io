(a GS[q].GS[l] )/.Dot -\> mydot /.
{a\*mydot[x\_\_\_, GS[l], y\_\_\_] :\>
mydot[x,GS[J],y]} /. mydot -\> Dot  

This has to do with the way the Mathematica pattern matcher works.

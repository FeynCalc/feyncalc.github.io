PaVeReduce

PaVeReduce[expr] reduces all Passarino-Veltman integrals (i.e. all PaVe’s) in expr down to scalar A0, B0, C0 and D0.

See also

Overview, FRH, PaVeOrder.

Examples

PaVeReduce[PaVe[1, 2, {s, m^2, m^2}, {m^2, m^2, M^2}], IsolateNames -> FF] 
 
FRH[%]

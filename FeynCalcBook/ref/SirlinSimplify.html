SirlinSimplify

SirlinSimplify[exp]
simplifies spinor chains that contain Dirac matrices using relations derived by Sirlin in Nuclear Physics B192 (1981) 93-99. Contrary to the original paper, the sign of the Levi-Civita tensor is choosen as ϵ0123=1 which is the standard choice in FeynCalc.

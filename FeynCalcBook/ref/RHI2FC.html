RHI

RHI[{v,w,x,y,z},{a,b,c,d,e,f,g}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}]
(sn  1, mark1  1, mark2  1, mark3  1, eph  Epsilon/2 ). The exponents of the numerator scalar product are (dl = OPEDelta): v: k1.k1, w: k2.k2, x: p.k1, y: p.k2, z: k1.k2. a: dl.k1, b: dl.k2, c: dl.(p-k1), d: dl.(p-k2), e: dl.(k1-k2), f: dl.(p+k1-k2), g: dl.(p-k1-k2) RHI[any___,{a,b,c,d,e,0,0}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}] simplifies to RHI[any, {a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}]; RHI[{0,0,0,0,0},{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}] simplifies to RHI[{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}].

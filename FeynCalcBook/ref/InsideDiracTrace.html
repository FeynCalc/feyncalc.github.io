InsideDiracTrace


is and option of DiracSimplify. If set to True, DiracSimplify assumes to operate inside a DiracTrace, i.e., products of an odd number of Dirac matrices are discarded. Furthermore simple traces are calculated (but divided by a factor 4, i.e. : DiracSimplify[DiracMatrix[a,b], InsideDiracTraceTrue] yields ScalarProduct[a,b]) Traces involving more than four DiracGamma's and DiracGamma[5] are not performed.

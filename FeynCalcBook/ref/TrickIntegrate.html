TrickIntegrate


does an integration trick for the definite integral of ( g[t]) from 0 to 1, yielding g[1]/a/Epsilon + Hold[Integrate][ (g[t]-g[1]),{t,0,1}]. TrickIntegrate[ g[t], t] gives + Hold[Integrate][ (g[t]-g[0]),{t,0,1}], provided g[1] and g[0] exist.

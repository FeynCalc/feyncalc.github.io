GSE

GSE[p]
can be used as input for a D-4-dimensional and is transformed into DiracGamma[Momentum[p,D-4],D-4] by FeynCalcInternal (=FCI). GSE[p,q, ...] is a short form for GSE[p].GSE[q]. ... .

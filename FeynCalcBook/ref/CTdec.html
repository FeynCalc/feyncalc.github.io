CTdec

CTdec[{{qi,a},{qj,b},...},{p1,p2,...}]
or CTdec[exp, {{qi, a}, {qj, b}, ...}, {p1, p2, ...}] calculates the tensorial decomposition formulas for Cartesian integrals. The more common ones are saved in TIDL.

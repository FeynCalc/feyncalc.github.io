SUNFDeltaContract

SUNFDeltaContract[exp]
substitues for all SUNFDelta in exp SUNFDeltaContract, contracts the fundamental SU(N) indices and resubstitutes SUNFDelta.

SUNFDeltaContract[i,j]
is the Kronecker-delta for SU(N) in the fundamental representation with contraction properties. It wraps the head SUNFIndex around its arguments.

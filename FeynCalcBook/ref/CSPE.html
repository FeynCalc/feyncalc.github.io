CSPE

CSPE[p,q]
is the D-4-dimensional scalar product of p with q and is transformed into CartesianPair[CartesianMomentum[p, D-4],CartesianMomentum[q, D-4]] by FeynCalcInternal. CSPE[p] is the same as CSPE[p,p] ( =p2).

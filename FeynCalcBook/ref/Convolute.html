Convolute


convolutes f(x) and g(x), i.e., Convolute[f, g] is equivalent to Convolute[f, g, x]. Convolute[exp, {x1, x2}] assumes that exp is polynomial in x1 and x2. Convolute uses table-look-up and does not do any integral calculations, only linear algebra.

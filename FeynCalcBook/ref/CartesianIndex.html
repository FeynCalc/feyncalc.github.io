CartesianIndex

CartesianIndex
is the head of Cartesian indices. The internal representation of a three-dimensional i is CartesianIndex[i]. For other than three dimensions: CartesianIndex[i, Dimension]. CartesianIndex[i, 3] simplifies to CartesianIndex[i]. The first argument cannot be an integer.

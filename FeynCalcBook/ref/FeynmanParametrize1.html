FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FeynmanParametrize1

FeynmanParametrize1[exp,k,Method->Denominator]
introduces Feynman parameters for all one-loop integrals in exp (k = integration momentum) using formula (11.A.1) from "The Quantum Theory of Fields" vol. 1 by Steven Weinberg. FeynmanParametrize1[exp,k,Method->Exp] introduces Feynman parameters for all one-loop integrals in exp (k = integration momentum) using 1/(A-I eps) = I Integrate[Exp[-I x (A-I eps)],{x,0,Infinity}, Assumptions->{Arg[A]==0,Arg[eps]==0}]. In this case, when the option Integrate is set to True, odd factors of k-tensors are dropped and even factors are replaced according to Itzykson&Zuber (8-117).

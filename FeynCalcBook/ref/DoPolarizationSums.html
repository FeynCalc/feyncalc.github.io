DoPolarizationSums

DoPolarizationSums[exp,k,n]
sums over physical (transverse) polarizations of an external massless vector boson with momentum k, where n is an auxiliary 4-vector from the gauge-dependent polarization sum formula.

DoPolarizationSums[exp,k,0]
replaces the polarization sum of an external massless vector boson with momentum k by -gμν. This corresponds to the summation over all four polarizations, including the unphysical ones.

DoPolarizationSums[exp,k]
sums over the three polarizations of an external massive vector boson with momentum k and mass k2.

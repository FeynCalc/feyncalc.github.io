FEYN CALC SYMBOL

DoPolarizationSums


sums over vector polarizations for expressions with a factor of the form Pair[LorentzIndex[rho1_], Momentum[Polarization[p_, -I]]]* Pair[LorentzIndex[rho2_], Momentum[Polarization[p_, I]]].

FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FCLoopBasisCreateScalarProducts

FCLoopBasisCreateScalarProducts{q1,q2,...},{p1,p2,...},{d1,d2,...},head]
generates a list of all loop-momentum dependent scalar products made out of the loop momenta q1, q2, ... and external momenta p1, p2, ... in the space-time dimensions d1, d2, .... The argument head can be Pair to generate Lorentzian scalar products or CartesianPair to generate Cartesian scalar products.

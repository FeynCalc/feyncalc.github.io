FEYN CALC SYMBOL

GenPaVe

GenPaVe[i,j,...,{{0,m0},{Momentum[p1],m1},{Momentum[p2],m2},...]
denotes the invariant (and scalar) Passarino-Veltman integrals, i.e. the coefficient functions of the tensor integral decomposition. In contrast to PaVe which uses the LoopTools convention, masses and external momenta in GenPaVe are written in the same order as they appear in the original tensor integral, i.e. FAD[{q,m0},{q-p1,m1},{q-p2,m2},...].

Spinor

Spinor[p, m, o]
is the head of Dirac spinors.

DetailsDetails

  • Which of the spinors u, v, or is understood, depends on the sign of the momentum (p) argument and the relative position in the chain.
  • Spinor[Momentum[p],m] means  if it stands at the beginning of the chain.
  • Spinor[Momentum[p],m] means  if it stands at the end of the chain.
  • Spinor[-Momentum[p],m] means  if it stands at the beginning of the chain.
  • Spinor[-Momentum[p],m] means  if it stands at the end of the chain.
  • Spinors of fermions of mass m are normalized to have square u=2 m and v=-2 m.
  • The optional argument o can be used for additional degrees of freedom. If no optional argument o is supplied, a 1 is subsituted in.

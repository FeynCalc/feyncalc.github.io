TrickMandelstam

TrickMandelstam[expr, {s, t, u,m1^2+m2^2+m3^2+m4^2}]
simplifies all sums in expr so that one of the Mandelstam variables s, t or u is eliminated by the relation s + t + u = + + + . The trick is that the resulting sum has the most short number of terms.

FeynAmp

FeynAmp[q, amp]
is the head of a Feynman amplitude, where amp denotes the analytical expression for the amplitude and q is the integration variable. FeynAmp[q1, q2, amp] denotes a two-loop amplitude.

DetailsDetails

  • FeynAmp has no functional properties and serves just as a head. There are however special typesetting rules attached.

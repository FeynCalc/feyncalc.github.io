Write2

Write2[file, val1 = expr1, val2 = expr2, ...]
writes the settings val1 = expr1, val2 = expr2 in sequence followed by a newline, to the specified output file.

DetailsDetails

  • Setting the option FormatType of Write2 to FortranForm results in Fortran syntax output.

FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FCCompareResults

FCCompareResults[{res1,res2,...},{res1Known,res2Known,...}]
compares the given list of expression {res1,res2,...} to the list of expressions {res1Known,res2Known,...} that represenet the correct results. This is handy for checking both intermediate and final results of calculations, where you know what should come out at the end.

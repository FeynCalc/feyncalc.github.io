TLI

TLI[{v,w,x,y,z},{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}]
the exponents of the numerator scalar product are (dl = OPEDelta): v: k1.k1, w: k2.k2, x: p.k1, y: p.k2, z: k1.k2. a: dl.k1, b: dl.k2, c: dl.(p-k1), d: dl.(p-k2), e: dl.(k1-k2). TLI[{mu1, ...}, {nu1, ...}][{v,w,x,y,z},{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}] is a tensor integral, where mu1 belongs to k1 and nu1 to k2. TLI[any___,{a,b,c,d,e,0,0}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}] simplifies to TLI[any, {a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}]. TLI[{0,0,0,0,0},{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}] simplifies to TLI[{a,b,c,d,e}, {al,be,ga,de,ep}].

QuantumField

QuantumField
is the head of quantized fields and their derivatives. QuantumField[par, ftype, {lorind}, {sunind}] denotes a quantum field of type ftype with (possible) Lorentz-indices lorind and SU(N) indices sunind. The optional first argument par denotes a partial derivative acting on the field.

DiracReduce

DiracReduce[exp]
reduces all 4-dimensional Dirac matrices in exp to the standard basis (S,P,V,A,T) using the Chisholm identity.

DetailsDetails

  • In the result the basic Dirac structures are wrapped with a head DiracBasis, that is

    • S: DiracBasis[1]

    P: DiracBasis[GA[5]]

    V:DiracBasis[GA[μ]]

    A:DiracBasis[GA[μ, 5]]

    T:DiracBasis[DiracSigma[GA[μ,ν]]]

  • By default DiracBasis is substituted to Identity.

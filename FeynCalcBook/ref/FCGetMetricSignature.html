FCGetMetricSignature

FCGetMetricSignature[]
returns the signature of the Minkowski metric used when working with Cartesian objects, like CartesianPair, CartesianIndex, CartesianMomentum etc. {1,-1} corresponds to (1,-1,-1,-1) and {-1,1} means (-1, 1, 1, 1).

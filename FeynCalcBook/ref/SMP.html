SMP

SMP["par"]
displays a symbol for the model parameter "par".

DetailsDetails

  • Typical parameters are masses, coupling constants, mixing angles etc.
  • Parameters that are complex, like CKM matrix element, have an I as an additional argument, i.e. SMP["V_ud",I] and SMP["V_ud",-I].
  • SMP[] shows the list of all available parameters.

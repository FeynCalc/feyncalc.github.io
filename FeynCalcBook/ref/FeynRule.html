FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FeynRule


derives the Feynman rule corresponding to the field configuration fields of the lagrangian lag. FeynRule does not calculate propagator Feynman rules. The option ZeroMomentumInsertion can be used for twist-2 and higher twist operators.

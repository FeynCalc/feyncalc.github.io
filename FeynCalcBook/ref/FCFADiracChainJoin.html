FCFADiracChainJoin

FCFADiracChainJoin[exp]
processes the output of FeynArts (after FCFAConvert) with explicit Dirac indices and joins matrices and spinors into closed chains. This is necessary e.g. for models with 4-fermion operators, where FeynArts cannot determine the correct relative signs. When two matrices have a common index but the positions do not match, as in AijBik, it is assumed that we can take the charge conjugate transposed of either matrix to obtain, e.g. (C ATC-1)jiBik or (C BTC-1)kiAij.

FCChargeConjugateTransposed

FCChargeConjugateTransposed[exp]
represents the application of the charge conjugation operator to the transposed of exp, i.e. C expT C-1. Here exp is understood to be a single Dirac matrix or a chain thereof.

DetailsDetails

  • The option setting Explicit determines whether the explicit result is returned or whether it is left in the unevaluated form.
  • The unevaluated form will be also maintained if the function does not know how to obtain C expT C-1 from the given exp.
  • The shortcut for FCChargeConjugateTransposed is FCCCT.

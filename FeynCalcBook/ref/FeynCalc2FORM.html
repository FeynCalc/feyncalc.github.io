FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FeynCalc2FORM


displays expr in FORM syntax. FeynCalc2FORM[file, x] writes x in FORM syntax to a file. FeynCalc2FORM[file, x==y] writes x=y to a file in FORM syntax.

Solve2

Solve2
is equivalent to Solve, except that it works only for linear equations (and returns just a list) and accepts the options Factoring and FinalSubstitutions. Solve2 uses the "high school algorithm" and factors intermediate results. Therefore it can be drastically more useful than Solve.

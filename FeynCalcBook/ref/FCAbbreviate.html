FCAbbreviate

FCAbbreviate[exp, {q1,q2,...},{p1,p2,...}]
introduces abbreivations for scalar products of external momenta, SMP-objects and other variables that are present in the expression.

DetailsDetails

  • Functions (LeafCount > 1) are not supported.
  • The main purpose is to simplify the export of FeynCalc expressions to other software tools that might not provide the richness of Mathematica's syntax
  • The result is returned as a list of replacement rules for scalar products, SMPs and all other variables present.

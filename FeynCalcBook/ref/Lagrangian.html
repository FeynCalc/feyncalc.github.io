Lagrangian


gives the unpolarized OPE quark operator. Lagrangian["oqp"] gives the polarized quark OPE operator. Lagrangian["ogu"] gives the unpolarized gluon OPE operator. Lagrangian["ogp"] gives the polarized gluon OPE operator. Lagrangian["ogd"] gives the sigma-term part of the QCD lagrangian. Lagrangian["QCD"] gives the gluon self interaction part of the QCD lagrangian.

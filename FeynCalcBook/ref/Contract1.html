FEYN CALC SYMBOL

Contract1


For covariant calculations it is not necessary to distinguish between co- and contravariant indices (except for the Levi-Civita tensor). Therefore normally no distinction is made between upper and lower indices. Howere, if needed, one can use Upper and Lower and then a distuction is made: Contract1[exp] contracts Upper and Lower indices. Neither Upper and Upper nor Lower and Lower indices are contracted.

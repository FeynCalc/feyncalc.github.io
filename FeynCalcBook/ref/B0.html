B0

B0[pp, ma^2, mb^2]
is the Passarino-Veltman two-point integral . All arguments are scalars and have dimension mass^2. If the option BReduce is set to True, certain B0's are reduced to A0's. Setting the option B0Unique to True simplifies B0[a,0,a] and B0[0,0,a].

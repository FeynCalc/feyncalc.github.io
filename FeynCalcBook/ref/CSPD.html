CSPD

CSPD[p,q]
is the D-1-dimensional scalar product of p with q and is transformed into CartesianPair[CartesianMomentum[p, D-1],CartesianMomentum[q, D-1]] by FeynCalcInternal. CSPD[p] is the same as CSPD[p,p] (=p2).

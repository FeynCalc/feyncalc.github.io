FEYN CALC SYMBOL

SUNSimplify


simplifies products of SUNT (and complex conjugated) matrices. Basic renaming of dummy indices is done. If the option SUNTrace is set to False, then any SUNT-matrices are taken out of DiracTrace[...]; otherwise a color-trace is taken (by SUNTrace) before taking the SUN-objects in front of DiracTrace[...].

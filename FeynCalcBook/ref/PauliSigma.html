FEYN CALC SYMBOL

PauliSigma

PauliSigma[x,dim]
is the internal representation of a Pauli matrix with a Lorentz or Cartesian index or a contraction of a Pauli matrix and a Lorentz or Cartesian vector. PauliSigma[x,3] simplifies to PauliSigma[x].

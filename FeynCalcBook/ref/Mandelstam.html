Mandelstam


is an option for DiracTrace, OneLoop, OneLoopSum, Tr and TrickMandelstam. A typical setting is Mandelstam  {s, t, u, m1^2+m2^2+m3^2+m4^2}, which implies s + t + u = m1^2+m2^2+m3^2+m4^2. If other than four-particle processes are calculated, the setting should be: Mandelstam  {}.

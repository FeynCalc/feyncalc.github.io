ScalarGluonVertex

ScalarGluonVertex[{p}, {q}, {μ,a}]
or ScalarGluonVertex[p, q, μ, a] yields the scalar-scalar-gluon vertex, where p and q are incoming momenta.

ScalarGluonVertex[{μ,a},{ν,b}]
yields the scalar-scalar-gluon-gluon vertex, where p and q are incoming momenta. syntax.

OPEIntegrateDelta

OPEIntegrateDelta[expr, x, m]
introduces the δ(1-x) (DeltaFunction[1-x]). The Mathematica Integrate function is called and each integration (from 0 to 1) is recorded for reference (and bug-checking) in the list $MIntegrate.

DetailsDetails

  • Notice that the dimension specified by the option should also be the dimension used in expr. It is replaced in OPEIntegrateDelta by (4+Epsilon).

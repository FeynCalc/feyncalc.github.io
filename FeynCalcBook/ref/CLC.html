CLC

CLC[m,n,r]
evaluates to Eps[CartesianIndex[m], CartesianIndex[n], CartesianIndex[r]] applying FeynCalcInternal. CLC[m,...][p, ...] evaluates to Eps[CartesianIndex[m], ..., CartesianMomentum[p], ...] applying FeynCalcInternal.

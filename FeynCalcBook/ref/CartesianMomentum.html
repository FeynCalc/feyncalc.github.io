CartesianMomentum

CartesianMomentum[p]
is the head of a three momentum (p). The internal representation of a three-dimensional p is CartesianMomentum[p]. For other than three dimensions: CartesianMomentum[p, Dimension]. CartesianMomentum[p, 3] simplifies to CartesianMomentum[p].

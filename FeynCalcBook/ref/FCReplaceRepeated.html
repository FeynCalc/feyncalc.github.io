FCReplaceRepeated

FCReplaceRepeated[exp,ru1,...]
is like ReplaceRepeated, but it also allows to apply multiple replacement rules sequentially. Instead of doing exp //. ru1 //. ru2 //. ru3 one can just write FCReplaceRepeated[exp, ru1, ru2, ru3].

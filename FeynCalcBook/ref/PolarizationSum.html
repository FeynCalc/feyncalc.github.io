PolarizationSum

PolarizationSum[μ,ν, ... ]
returns different polarization sums depending on its arguments

DetailsDetails

  • PolarizationSum[μ, ν] or PolarizationSum[μ, ν, k, 0] gives -gμν.
  • PolarizationSum[μ, ν, k] returns -gμν+.
  • PolarizationSum[μ, ν, k, n] yields -gμν+-.

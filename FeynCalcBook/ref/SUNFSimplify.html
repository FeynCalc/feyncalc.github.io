SUNFSimplify

SUNFSimplify[exp]
is an auxiliary function that simplifies expressions containing SU(N) indices in the fundamental representation.

DetailsDetails

  • The simplifications performed by SUNFSimplify are mostly limited to the contractions of the fundamental indices. The function is by far not as powerful as SUNSimplify.

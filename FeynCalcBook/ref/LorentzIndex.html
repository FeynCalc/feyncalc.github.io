FEYN CALC SYMBOL

LorentzIndex


denotes a four dimensional Lorentz index. For other than four dimensions: LorentzIndex[mu, D] or LorentzIndex[mu] etc. LorentzIndex[mu, 4] simplifies to LorentzIndex[mu].

FEYN CALC SYMBOL

SUNDeltaContract


substitues for all SUNDelta in expr SUNDeltaContract, contracts the SUN(N) indices and resubstitutes SUNDelta. SUNDeltaContract[i, j] is the Kronecker-delta for SU(N) with contraction properties. SUNDeltaContract wraps also the head SUNIndex around its arguments.

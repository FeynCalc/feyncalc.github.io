SUNDeltaContract

SUNDeltaContract[exp]
substitues for all SUNDelta in exp SUNDeltaContract, contracts the adjoint SU(N) indices and resubstitutes SUNDelta.

SUNDeltaContract[i,j]
is the Kronecker-delta for SU(N) in the adjoint representation with contraction properties. It wraps the head SUNIndex around its arguments.

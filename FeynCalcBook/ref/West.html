FEYN CALC SYMBOL

West


West is an option for DiracTrace, TR and other functions that deal with traces of Dirac matrices. It applies only to the computation of D-dimensional chiral traces (i.e. those that involve one ) in the Breitenlohner-Maison-t'Hooft-Veltman (BMHV) scheme. If set to True, such traces will be computed according to formula (A.5) from Comp. Phys. Comm 77 (1993) 286-298, which is also known as West's formula.

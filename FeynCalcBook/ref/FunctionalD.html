FunctionalD

FunctionalD[exp,{QuantumField[name,LorentzIndex[mu],...,SUNIndex[a]][p],...}]
calculates the functional derivative of exp with respect to the QuantumField list (with incoming momenta p, etc.) and does the Fourier transform.

FunctionalD[expr,{QuantumField[name,LorentzIndex[mu],... SUNIndex[a]],...}]
calculates the functional derivative and does partial integration but omits the x-space delta functions.

DetailsDetails

  • FunctionalD is a low level function used in FeynRule.

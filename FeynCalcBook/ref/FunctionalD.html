FEYN CALC SYMBOL

FunctionalD


calculates the functional derivative of expr with respect to the field list (with incoming momenta p etc.) and does the fourier transform. FunctionalD[expr, {QuantumField[name, {mu},{a}], ...}] calculates the functional derivative and does partial integration but omits the x-space delta functions. FunctionalD is a low level function used in FeynRule.

DiracGammaExpand

DiracGammaExpand[exp]
expands Dirac matrices contracted to linear combinations of 4-vectors.

DetailsDetails

  • All DiracGamma[Momentum[a+b+..]] will be expanded to (DiracGamma[Momentum[a]]+DiracGamma[Momentum[b]]+...).

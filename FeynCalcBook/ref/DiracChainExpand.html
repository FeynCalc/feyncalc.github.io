DiracChainExpand

DiracChainExpand[exp]
expands all Dirac chains with explicit indices using linearity, e.g. DCHN[GA[p1]+GA[p2]+m,i,j] becomes DCHN[GA[p1],i,j]+DCHN[GA[p2],i,j]+m*DCHN[1,i,j]

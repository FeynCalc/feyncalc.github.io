CartesianPair

CartesianPair[a,b]
is a special pairing used in the internal representation.

DetailsDetails

  • a and b may have heads CartesianIndex or CartesianMomentum.
  • If both a and b have head CartesianIndex, the Kronecker delta is understood.
  • If a and b have head CartesianMomentum, a Cartesian scalar product is meant.
  • If one of a and b has head CartesianIndex and the other CartesianMomentum, a Cartesian vector pi is understood.

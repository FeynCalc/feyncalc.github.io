FC2TLI

FC2TLI[exp, k1, k2]
transforms all 2-loop OPE-integrals in FeynAmpDenominator form to the TLI-integrals. The option IncludePair governs the inclusion of scalar products p.k1, p.k2 and k1.k2 (setting True).

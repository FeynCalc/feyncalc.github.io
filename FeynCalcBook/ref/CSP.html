CSP

CSP[p,q]
is the 3-dimensional scalar product of p with q and is transformed into CartesianPair[CartesianMomentum[p],CartesianMomentum[q]] by FeynCalcInternal. CSP[p] is the same as CSP[p,p] (=p2).

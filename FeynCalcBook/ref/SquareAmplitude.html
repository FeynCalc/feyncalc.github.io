SquareAmplitude

SquareAmplitude[m1, m2]
multiplies the amplitudes from the list m1 with their complex conjugate from the list m2 to obtain the list of products m1im2j.

  • This function can be useful when exporting amplitudes obtained with FeynCalc to FORM.

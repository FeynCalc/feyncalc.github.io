Series2

Series2
performs a series expansion around 0. Series2 is (up to the Gamma-bug in Mathematica versions smaller than 5.0) equivalent to Series, except that it applies Normal on the result and has an option FinalSubstitutions. Series2[f, e, n] is equivalent to Series2[f, {e, 0, n}].
